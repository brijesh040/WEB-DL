Matt lamination is ideal for document
covers and posters. It helps to enhance
colour and provide extra durability to
documents and posters

Gloss lamination helps bring the colour to life and
make it 'pop'. Gloss is ideal for photo heavy artwork
and book covers.

Soft touch lamination is a high-end finish,
providing a velvety feel to your loose sheets
or document covers. It's a great choice for
high end flyers, save the dates, wedding invites
and book covers.
